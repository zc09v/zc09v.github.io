StowKey — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

StowKey is a local-only password manager. Your data never leaves your device. We operate on a strict zero-network, zero-collection principle.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

StowKey does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any data from your password vault
  • Any data at all

3. How Your Data is Stored

All vault data is stored exclusively on your device:

Data TypeStorage LocationEncryption
Passwords, entries, tags, spacesSQLite database in app sandboxAES-256-GCM at rest
Master password verification tokenmacOS / iOS KeychainSystem-level encryption
Key derivation saltmacOS / iOS KeychainSystem-level encryption
Guide / tutorial shown statusUserDefaults (boolean flags)None (non-sensitive)
  • The master password is never stored. It is only used in memory during an active session to derive encryption keys.
  • When you lock the vault, the session key is purged from memory immediately.
  • All encryption and key derivation happens on-device using Apple's CryptoKit and Argon2id.

4. Network Access

StowKey has zero network capability. The app:

  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not make any HTTP / HTTPS requests
  • Does not use cloud synchronization
  • Has macOS sandbox enabled with no network entitlements

This is by design: your passwords should never leave your device.

5. Third-Party Services

StowKey does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services. No third party has access to your data because no data is collected in the first place.

6. Export and Backup

You may export your vault as an encrypted .crhino file or an unencrypted .csv file. You control entirely where these files are saved and who has access to them. StowKey does not transmit exported files to any server.

7. Data Deletion

All vault data can be permanently deleted at any time:

  • Reset Account in Settings: deletes all spaces, entries, tags, and the verification token from Keychain
  • Manual deletion: simply uninstall the app and delete all data from the app sandbox

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered. There are no remote copies.

8. Children's Privacy

StowKey does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. It is compliant with COPPA and GDPR age requirements by design.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access, deletion, and portability. Since StowKey stores all data locally and does not collect or process any personal data remotely, you exercise these rights directly on your device:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Delete: Use "Reset Account" in Settings
  • Export: Use the Export feature to get a .crhino or .csv backup

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected in the app and on the associated website. Significant changes will be communicated via app update notes.
